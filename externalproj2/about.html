About Us

Welcome to Washed n' Shined, Where Quality Meets Convenience!

At Washed n' Shined, we’re passionate about keeping your car clean, shiny, and in top condition. We’re more than just a car wash—we’re a team dedicated to delivering exceptional service with every visit. Whether it’s a quick exterior wash or a full detailing, our goal is to make your car look and feel brand new, every time.

Our Story:

Founded in 2002, Washed n' Shined was born out of a desire to offer high-quality car care with a focus on convenience and customer satisfaction. As a locally owned and operated business, we take pride in our reputation for providing professional, affordable, and eco-friendly services. Over the years, we’ve grown to become a trusted name in the community, known for our attention to detail and commitment to excellence.

Our Mission:

We are dedicated to offering top-notch car cleaning services that not only meet, but exceed our customers' expectations. By using the latest technology and eco-conscious products, we ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible. Our mission is simple: to provide a fast, affordable, and eco-friendly solution to keep your car looking its best all year round.

  • Eco-Friendly Practices: We believe in taking care of your car and the environment. We use water-efficient systems and environmentally safe products to minimize our footprint and deliver a top-tier service.
  • Commitment to Convenience: Whether you’re visiting our location or taking advantage of our mobile services, we make it easy for you to get your car cleaned, without the hassle.
